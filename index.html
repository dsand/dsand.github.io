Welcome to Team Sand!

We are a multi-pronged observational astrophysics group, studying the biggest explosions in the universe, and the smallest dark matter halos.

If you are an undergraduate looking for a research opportunity, please contact me. No real experience is necessary. All I expect is enthusiasm and reliability.

Team Sand will be hiring postdoctoral researchers in the areas of time domain astrophysics and nearby galaxies during the Fall 2023-Winter 2024 cycle. Feel free to reach out, and the job ad will be posted in October or November 2023.
